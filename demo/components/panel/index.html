Panel

The confirmation panel has a turquoise background and white text. Used for transaction end pages, and Bank Holidays.

The information panel has a GOV.UK blue background and white text. Used for explanatory pages.

Guidance

Guidance and documentation can be found on GOV.UK Design System.
